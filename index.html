Frequently Asked Questions

                        

                            

                                
How do I add a new game?

                                
Go to the "Add Game" tab, fill in the required information, and click "Generate Game Code".

                            

                            

                                
Can I edit existing games?

                                
Yes, go to the "Game Library" tab, find the game you want to edit, and click the "Edit" button.

                            

                            

                                
How do I export my game data?

                                
In the "Game Library" tab, click the "Export" button to download your game data as a JSON file.
